Orders

Manage customer orders including regular orders and Mega Buy group orders. Track order status from Draft to Delivered or Closed states.

Everyone creates normal orders in Draft state.

Mega order can be created when there are more than 2 normal orders, we can adjust normal order and recalculated mega order to meet minimum requirement for purchase.

Once mega order is placed to store, normal order will be locked and cannot be edited anymore, but they can be edited again after delivery to update with actual quantity and price.

Mega order can also be recalculated after delivery to adjust with actual quantity and price.
